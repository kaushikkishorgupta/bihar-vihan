Top Favorite Destinations in Bihar

Handpicked spiritual, heritage, and nature destinations every Bihar traveler should explore.

Bodh Gaya

A UNESCO World Heritage site and one of the most sacred Buddhist pilgrimage destinations in the world. The Mahabodhi Temple marks the place where Lord Buddha attained enlightenment.

Explore More

Nalanda University Ruins

Ancient world-famous university and a major center of learning in history. The archaeological remains reflect India’s rich educational heritage.

Explore More

Rajgir

A historic city surrounded by hills, known for Vishwa Shanti Stupa, hot springs, and ropeway experience. Important in both Buddhist and Jain history.

Explore More

Vaishali

One of the world’s first republics and an important site in Buddhist and Jain traditions. Known for Ashokan Pillar and peaceful surroundings.

Explore More

Patna Sahib (Takht Sri Harmandir Sahib)

Birthplace of Guru Gobind Singh Ji and an important Sikh pilgrimage site with beautiful architecture.

Explore More

About us

Bihar Vihaan was founded on 1st April 2023 by Kaushal Kishor Gupta with a bold vision: to illuminate the rich, vibrant, and often overlooked culture, history, tourism, and talent of Bihar.

A New Beginning

"Vihaan" means a new beginning-and that's what we strive to bring for Bihar through the power of digital media, community-driven content, and technological innovation.

Our Platform

Based in Patna, our platform is not just another media outlet-it is a movement, a voice for the people, and a stage for talent that mainstream media often ignores.
